About Us

For Energy Professionals and Investors, Eye on Western CanadaTM delivers products to monitor Oil and Gas activity across the Western Canadian Sedimentary Basin. Our current content includes licensing, drilling activity, and crown land sale maps with associated geological edges and interactive data dashboards. Our source database files are available to purchase through monthly subscriptions or single transactions.
